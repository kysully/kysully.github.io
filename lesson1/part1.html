Lesson 1 - Part 1: Hello World

It is a standard tradition among the world of computer programmers to introduce new users to their first "Hello World" program. So let's dive in!

Before we get started, let's take a look at a variable. A variable is a piece of data the exists within the realm of the program. In our example, we will make a new variable named x, using the keyword var.
